    About Us

    Bringing loving care to health care

    Marina Pharmacy Group is a well established retail healthcare provider for over 14 years in the market, well-known and recognised, foraiming at providing optimal pharmacy healthcare services, in the same time following up-to-date international and local standards. We're also an acknowledged sub-agent and distributor to retail pharmacies in Bahrain. We have currently six points of sales, distributed allover Bahrain. In addition, we have a big warehouse, which is very well equipped to store medicines, herbs, cosmetics and medical devices, and is licensed from the NHRA.

    Our Services

    Retail and Wholesale

    Since 2009, we continuously provide retail & wholesale of pharmaceutical and wellness products. We started as one branch in Riffa, Bahrain. Now we are a proud group of six branches, and still to come.

    Delivery to Our Clients

    The world is changing, & we are adapting well. It is very well-known that e-shopping is an increasing trend. Thus we provide a motorpool of new cars to deliver products safely & quickly to all Bahrain.

    Import and Distribution

    We already have distributed many of pharmaceutical & cosmetic products for years. Now we are opening the door to import our own high quality products from trusted & internationally renowned producers.

    Indirect Patient Care

    We don't just sell medicine, but we serve also our customers with indirect patient care, as current local laws & regulations allow. We measure heart pressure, body-temperature, -weight, -length & much more.

    Advise and Consulting

    We care for our customers, so we choose our team carefully to reach zero-errors in all our operations, from ordering, purchasing, storing, delivering, & advising customers with best tips & counseling.

    24/7 Outlets

    We serve our valued customers all over the kingdom of Bahrain through our six outlets distributed wisely in the country, from 7:00 am to 3:00 am. Our branch, the heart of Juffair will open very soon 24/7.

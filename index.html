    About Marina Pharmacy

    Bringing loving care to health care

    Marina Pharmacy Group is a well established retail healthcare provider for over 14 years in the Bahraini market, well-known and recognised, foraiming at providing optimal pharmacy healthcare services and in the same time following up-to-date international and local standards. We're also an acknowledged sub-agent and distributor to retail pharmacies in Bahrain.

    Our Services

    Retail and Wholesale

    Since 2009, we continuously provide retail & wholesale of pharmaceutical and wellness products. We started as one branch in Riffa, Manama and now we are a proud group of six branches, and still to come.

    Delivery to Our Clients

    The world is changing, & we are adapting well. It is very well-known that e-shopping is an increasing trend. Thus we provide a motorpool of new cars to deliver products safely & quickly to all Bahrain.

    Import and Distribution

    We already have distributed many of pharmaceutical & cosmetic products for years. Now we are opening the door to import our own high quality products from trusted & internationally renowed producers.

    Indirect Patient Care

    We don't just sell medicine, but we serve also our customers with indirect patient care, as current local laws & regulations allow. We measure heart pressure, body-temperature, -weight, -length & much more.

    Advise and Consulting

    We care for our customers, so we choose our team carefully to reach zero-errors in all our operations, from ordering, purchasing, storing, delivering, & advising customers with best tips & counseling.

    24/7 Outlets

    We serve our valued customers all over the kingdom of Bahrain through our six outlets distributed wisely in the country, from 7:00 am to 2:00 am. Our branch in Juffair will open very soon 24/7.
